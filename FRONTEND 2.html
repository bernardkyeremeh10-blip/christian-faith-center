About Our Church

We are a community of believers committed to following Jesus and serving our community

Our Mission & Vision

At Christian Faith Centre, our mission is to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ. We believe in the power of God's love to transform lives and communities.

Founded in 1992, we have been serving the local community for decades now, sharing the hope of the Gospel through word and action.

Our vision is to see lives transformed, families restored, and communities renewed through the power of the Holy Spirit.
